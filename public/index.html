About the Studio

Design with intention.
Style with precision.

LS Curated Design, LLC is a St. Louis-based interior design studio dedicated to creating spaces that are both beautiful and deeply personal. Every project begins with listening — understanding your lifestyle, your vision, and what home truly means to you.

I specialize in elevating your space.....one room at a time. Call for any questions!

Curated Aesthetic Every element selected with purpose — nothing excess, nothing missing.
Client-Centered Process Your vision drives every decision from the first consultation to final install.
Full-Service Execution From mood boards to sourcing to styling — handled with care.
What We Offer

Services designed to elevate

Whether you need a single room refresh or a full home transformation, LS Curated Design delivers an experience as refined as the result.

01
Interior Design Consultation

A focused session to assess your space, discuss your goals, and develop a clear direction for your project. Ideal for homeowners who need expert guidance before committing to a full redesign.

02
Full Room Design

Complete design of one or more rooms including layout planning, furniture selection, color palette, lighting, textiles, and accessories — all sourced and coordinated for you.

03
Space Planning

Strategic analysis and redesign of your floor plan to maximize flow, function, and visual impact without changing your architecture.

04
Color Consultation

Expert color strategy for your walls, finishes, fabrics, and accents. Achieve the mood you want — calm, bold, warm, dramatic — with confidence.

05
Furniture & Decor Sourcing

Access to trade-exclusive vendors and curated product recommendations. We handle procurement, tracking, and delivery coordination so you don’t have to.

06
Staging & Styling

Professional home staging for sale, rental, or simply elevating your everyday environment. Thoughtful arrangement and accessory styling that photographs beautifully.

How It Works

A seamless process,
start to finish.

Four clear steps from your first inquiry to a fully transformed space.

01
Discovery Call

We start with a conversation about your space, your style, and your goals. No commitment — just clarity.

02
Design Concept

A custom mood board, layout plan, and curated selections are presented for your feedback and approval.

03
Sourcing & Procurement

Once approved, we source every piece, manage orders, and coordinate deliveries on your behalf.

04
Installation & Reveal

We install, style, and present your finished space — ready for you to enjoy the moment you walk in.
